Road condition

We are building a global network where every vehicle is connected. Each one notifies the other participants when and where it encounters a pothole, steep terrain, damaged, wet or icy roads, and even when its safety systems were activated like ABS, traction control, etc.

Gain a third sense while driving with our app. Earn $SENSE tokens by contributing.

Navigation

Use the built-in navigation which gives you not only the shortest but also the safest route. It provides you with a great user experience by highlighting in different colors the road sections depending on their safety rating. Receive notifications about real-time events like crashes, traffic jams, weather, and road conditions on your route.

 Data

Our road safety algorithm is so efficient because it has access to a vast array of different data sources. Some of these are: systems diagnostics from inside the vehicle, weather conditions, public data provided by the local authority like construction and maintenance sites as well as places of frequent car crashes.

You can help expand our network by connecting your vehicle as well.
